Transaction Monetization (Phase 2/3)

Once you add escrow/orders:

Success fee (only when transaction happens)

Choose one:

0.75%–1.5% platform fee (cap at ₹50k)or

Flat fee by ticket size (e.g., ₹2,999 for <₹5L, ₹7,999 for ₹5–25L, etc.)

Escrow fee

Pass-through + small markup, or

0.25% escrow handling

This is where revenue scales without subscription pressure.



The “Most Profitable” Packaging Strategy

Your best packaging isn’t “more features.” It’s more outcomes:

Outcome bundles you should advertise

More visibility → Featured, Hub placement

More trust → Verified badges + certificates

More speed → RFQ priority + response-time boosts

More control → Team seats + workflows + exports

More insight → Analytics + demand heatmaps
